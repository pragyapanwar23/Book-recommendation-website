About Non-Fiction Books

Non-fiction books are literary works that present factual information, ideas, or arguments about real-world subjects. Unlike fiction, which is primarily based on the author's imagination, non-fiction books are grounded in reality and aim to inform, educate, or persuade readers about various topics, events, or phenomena.
