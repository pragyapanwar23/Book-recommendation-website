About Mystery Books

Mystery books are a genre of fiction that revolves around the investigation and resolution of a puzzle or crime. These books typically feature a central mystery or enigma that drives the plot, often involving a crime such as murder, theft, or kidnapping. Mystery novels are characterized by their suspenseful atmosphere, intricate plots, and the gradual revelation of clues and suspects leading to the resolution of the mystery.
